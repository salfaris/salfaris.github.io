Hi, I'm Salman but you can call me Sal.

I'm currently studying for an MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Oxford. I recently graduated from King's College London with a BSc in Mathematics.

Previously, I was an Undergraduate Research Fellow at the Department of Mathematics, King's College London, under the supervision of Dr. Izaak Neri. There, I worked on exploring a new method to visualise large food webs, and in more generality, large complex networks.

I am very passionate about data science and machine learning . In particular, I love tackling difficult time series analysis problems using ML approaches among many others.

When I'm not writing code, you can find me playing table tennis or poker in the MCR.

My tech stack includes:

  • Programming language: Python, C/C++
  • Data analysis stack: R, SQL, NumPy, pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • ML stack: scikit-learn, PyTorch
  • Web and mobile stack: HTML/CSS/JavaScript, Flask (Python), SwiftUI
  • Others: Git, LaTeX, NetworkX
