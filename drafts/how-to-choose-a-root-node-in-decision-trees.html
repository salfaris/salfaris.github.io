How to choose a Root Node in Decision Trees?

Posted on: Wed 26 May 2021

If you've watched Youtube videos or read articles about Decision Trees, the idea behind how Decision Trees work are quite intuitive. At every level of the tree, you choose a feature that is most significant in segregating the target value; and you keep on doing this until you reach a certain criteria (usually when you reach a certain number of minimum leaves). So conceptually it is straightforward, but when you start to have a go at doing it – actually computing and choosing the feature at each level – it's not as straightforward anymore because knowing where to start could be quite daunting given all the data. This post today is focused on guiding you how to do exactly this. Together, we will explore how to compute and choose a root node for a decision tree, and we will see that this is the only knowledge you would need to build a whole decision tree.
